Call of Duty: Infinite Warfare System Requirements

Minimum:

  • Operating System: Windows 7 64-Bit (Service Pack 1), Windows 8.1 64-Bit, Windows 10 64-Bit
  • Processor: Intel Core i3-3225 @ 3.30GHz or AMD FX-8350 @ 4.0GHz
  • Memory: 8 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 660 2GB / AMD Radeon HD 7850 2GB
  • DirectX: Version 11
  • Network: Broadband Internet Connection
  • Storage: 70 GB available space
  • Sound Card: DirectX 11 Compatible

Recommended:

  • Operating System: Windows 10 64-Bit
  • Processor: Intel Core i5-4690 @ 3.50GHz or AMD Ryzen 5 1600X @ 3.6GHz
  • Memory: 12 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 970 4GB / AMD Radeon R9 390 4GB
  • DirectX: Version 11
  • Network: Broadband Internet Connection
  • Storage: 70 GB available space
  • Sound Card: DirectX 11 Compatible

Additional Notes:
Online multiplayer requires an internet connection and a paid subscription service on some platforms.
Ensure you have the latest drivers for your graphics card and sound card.
Check your PC's specifications through tools like dxdiag.exe or System Information.
